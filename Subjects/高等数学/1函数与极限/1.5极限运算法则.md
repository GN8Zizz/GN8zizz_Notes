-  $\left\{\begin{array}{l} 双侧极限 \hspace{11.5mm}\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}f(x) \\ 右极限 \hspace{16.5mm} \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0^+}f(x) \\ 左极限 \hspace{16.5mm} \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0^-}f(x) \\ 无穷大极限\hspace{6.5mm}\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}f(x) \\ 正无穷大极限 \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow +\infty}f(x) \\ 负无穷大极限 \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty}f(x) \end{array}\right.$

## **[[1.4无穷小与无穷大|函数极限与无穷小的关系]]**
- $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow {\mathbb X}}f(x) = A \Leftrightarrow f(x) = A + \alpha$, 其中 $\alpha$ 满足 $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow {\mathbb X}}\alpha = 0$

## **无穷小的运算法则**
### 定理1
- 两个无穷小的和是无穷小
	若 $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}\alpha = 0,\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}\beta = 0$, 则 $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}(\alpha + \beta) = 0$
	- 证明:
#### 推论:
- 有限个无穷小的和也是无穷小
- 无限个无穷小的和不一定是无穷小
### 定理2
- 有界函数与无穷小的乘积是无穷小
	若 $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0} = 0$, $u$ 在 $x_0$ 的某个去心邻域内有界, 即 $u$ 局部有界
	则 $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}u \cdot \alpha = 0$
	- 证明:
#### 推论:
- 常数与无穷小的乘积是无穷小
- 两个无穷小的乘积是无穷小
- 有限个无穷小的乘积是无穷小
#### 常见有界函数:
- $\left\{\begin{array}{l} |{\rm sin }x| \le 1, \hspace{20.5mm} x \in (-\infty,+\infty) \\ |{\rm cos }x| \le 1, \hspace{20mm} x \in (-\infty,+\infty) \\ |{\rm arctan }x| \le \frac{\pi}{2}, \hspace{11.5mm} x \in (-\infty,+\infty) \\ 0 < |{\rm arccot }x| < \pi, \hspace{4.5mm} x \in (-\infty,+\infty)\end{array}\right.$

## **极限的四则运算法则**
### 定理3
- 若 $\displaystyle \lim f(x) = A,\lim f(x) = B$, 则
	1. $\lim [f(x) \pm g(x)] = A \pm B$
	2. $\lim [f(x) \cdot g(x)] = A \cdot B$
	3. 若 $\lim g(x) = B \ne 0$, 则 $\lim \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{\lim f(x)}{\lim g(x)} = \frac{A}{B}$
	- 证明:
		1. 
		2. 
		3. 
- 注:
	- 前提: $\lim f(x)、\lim g(x)$ 存在
	- 可推广到<mark class="hltr-grey">有限个</mark>函数极限运算的情形
#### 推论:
- 若 $\lim f(x)$ 存在, $C$ 为常数, 则 $\lim [C \cdot f(x)] = C \cdot \lim f(x)$
- 若 $\lim f(x)$ 存在, $n$ 是正整数, 则 $\lim[f(x)]^n = [\lim f(x)]^n$
### 定理4
- 设有数列 ${x_n}、{y_n}$, 若 $\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}x_n = A、\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}y_n = B$, 则
	1. $\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}(x_n \pm y_n) = A\pm B$
	2. $\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}(x_n \cdot y_n) = A \cdot B$
	3. 当 $y_n \ne 0(n = 1,2,...)$ 且 $B \ne 0$ 时, $\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{x_n}{y_n} = \frac{A}{B}$
### 定理5: 极限的保序性
- 若 $\varphi(x) \ge \Psi(x)$, 且 $\lim \varphi(x) = A,\lim \Psi(x) = B$, 则 $A \ge B$

## **多项式(有理整函数) 的极限**
### 定义:
- 形如 $f(x) = a_0x^n + a_1x^{n-1} + a_2x^{n-2} + ... +a_{n-1}x + a_n$ 称为 $n$ 次多项式($a_0 \ne 0,n \in {\mathbb N}$)
    1. $x\rightarrow x_0$ 时
		$\left.\begin{array}{l} \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}f(x) &= \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0} [a_0x^n + a_1x^{n-1} + a_2x^{n-2} + ... +a_{n-1}x + a_n] \\ &= \hspace{10mm} a_0 x_0^n + a_1 x_0^{n-1} + a_2 x_0^{n-2} + ... + a_{n-1} x_0 + a_n \\ &= f(x_0) \end{array}\right.$
	 2. $x \rightarrow \infty$ 时: 抓大头
		$\left.\begin{array}{l} \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}f(x) &= \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} [a_0x^n + a_1x^{n-1} + a_2x^{n-2} + ... +a_{n-1}x + a_n] \\ &= \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} x^n [a_0+ \frac{a_1}{x} + \frac{a_2}{x^2} + ... + \frac{a_{n-1}}{x^{n-1}} + \frac{a_n}{x^n} ]\\ &= a_0 \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}x^n \\ &= \infty \end{array}\right.$
## **有理分式函数的极限**
### 定义:
- 设 $P_m(x)、Q_n(x)$ 是多项式, 称 $F(x) = \frac{P_m(x)}{Q_n(x)}$ 为有理分式
	1. $x \rightarrow x_0$ 时 
		$\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}F(x) = \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0}\frac{P_m(x)}{Q_n(x)} \left\{\begin{array}{l} \frac{P_m(x_0)}{Q_n(x_0)} \hspace{15mm}, Q_n(x_0)\ne 0\\ \infty \hspace{23mm}, Q_n(x_0) = 0,P_m(x_0)\ne 0 \\ \frac{0}{0} \hspace{24mm} ,Q_n(x_0) = P_m(x_0) = 0 \end{array}\right.$
	2. $x \rightarrow \infty$
		$\left.\begin{array}{l} \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}F(x)  &= \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}\frac{P_m(x)}{Q_n(x)} \\ &= \displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{a_0x^m + a_1x^{m-1} + a_2x^{m-2} + ... +a_{m-1}x + a_m}{b_0x^n + b_1x^{n-1} + b_2x^{n-2} + ... +b_{n-1}x + b_n} ,a_0 \ne 0,b_0 \ne 0 \\ &=\left\{\begin{array}{l} \frac{a_0}{b_0} \hspace{19.5mm} ,m=n \\ \infty \hspace{20mm} ,m>n \\ 0 \hspace{22.5mm} ,m<n \end{array}\right. \end{array}\right.$

## **复合函数的极限运算法则**
### 定理6
- 设 $y = f[g(x)]$ 是由 $u = g(x)、y = f(x)$ 复合而成, $f[g(x)]$ 在 $x_0$ 的某去心邻域内有定义, 
    $\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0} g(x) = u_0、\displaystyle \lim_{u \rightarrow u_0} f(u) = A$
    且存在 $\delta > 0$, 当 $x \in \overset{\circ}{U}(x_0,\delta_0)$ 时 $g(x) \ne u_0$, 则 $$\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0} f[g(x)] = A$$
- 注:
	若 $g(x)、f(x)$ 满足定理6条件, 则
	$$\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow x_0} f[g(x)] \underset{x\rightarrow x_0,u\rightarrow u_0}{\overset{u = g(x)}{ =}} \displaystyle \lim_{u \rightarrow u_0} f(u)$$
